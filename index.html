News

(KTVI)- The Fountain on Locust claims to be the most photographed restaurant in St. Louis. Joy Christensen, from The Fountain on Locust, talks about all of the unique elements of the ice cream parlor. They create their own delicious and unique, made-from-scratch foods, as well as handcrafted ice cream sauces, chocolates and ice cream martinis. Whether you`re there for lunch, dinner, business or romance, be sure to try one of their signature Ice Cream Martinis, Retro Cocktails or Champagne Floats. In their west side booths you can listen to the only Restaurant Radio Comedy Serial, ‘Soap Hospital,’ with a new episode every two minutes! The Fountain on Locust is located on 3037 Locust, St. Louis, Mo. 63103. For more information visit fountainonlocust.com or call 314-535-7800.
