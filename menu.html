Come visit The Fountain on Locust - the most photographed restaurant in St Louis, with a one-of-a-kind hand painted art deco interior surrounding the entire dining room! What makes this unique cafe restaurant and ice cream bar a must visit in St. Louis? Besides the beautiful retro restaurant interior, The Fountain on Locust is the Home of The Ice Cream Martini, exceptional soups, cafe style food, hand crafted cocktails and amazing ice cream desserts.
